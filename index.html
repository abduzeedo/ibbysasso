Ibby Sasso

With over 15 years in public relations, Ibby has a diverse background, working with non-profits like Creative Growth Art Center and major retail brands such as Gap and Levi's. She's contributed to the household CPG brand Method and has agency experience with KCD, managing high-profile accounts including the CFDA and Topshop's U.S. launch. Her public affairs expertise includes promoting Kenneth Cole's social activism and collaborations with amfAR. Ibby excels in crafting tailored communication strategies, integrating media relations, partnerships, brand launches, influencer relations, email marketing, and reputation management.
