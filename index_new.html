Ibby has over 15 years of experience in public relations, working across various sectors including non-profits like Creative Growth Art Center, global consumer retail brands (Gap, Levi's), and CPG brand Method. Her time in Public Affairs included managing Kenneth Cole's appearances for social activism, in partnership with amfAR. Ibby specializes in creating customized communications strategies for clients, encompassing media relations, partnerships, brand launches, influencer relations, email marketing, and reputation strategy.
