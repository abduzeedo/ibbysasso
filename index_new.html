Ibby Sasso

Ibby started her career in public relations over 15 years ago. Her broad experience spans various sectors, from non-profits like Creative Growth Art Center to in-house roles at global consumer retail brands such as Gap and Levi's, along with household CPG brand Method. Additionally, Ibby has top-tier agency experience at KCD, where she managed accounts, including the CFDA (Council of Fashion Designers of America), and oversaw the stateside launch of the UK brand Top Shop. Ibby also spent time in the Public Affairs sector, managing appearances made by Kenneth Cole on behalf of his social activism and cause-related work in partnership with amfAR (the American Foundation for AIDS Research). Ibby takes a strategic and targeted approach to building a tailored communications strategy for each client, which includes media relations, partnerships, brand launches, influencer relations, email marketing, and reputation strategy.
